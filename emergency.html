FastDispatch Emergency Training Simulator

About

I am a Computer Engineering-focused student and TritonHacks 3rd place winner who built FastDispatch, an AI-powered emergency training simulator.

This project simulates realistic dispatcher scenarios with timed decisions and AI-driven conversation practice to help users train under pressure.

FastDispatch helps users practice high-pressure emergency dispatch decision-making with scenario logic, chatbot interactions, and feedback loops built for learning.
